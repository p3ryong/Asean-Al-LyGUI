The Cherished Daughter

The poem titled 'The Cherished Daughter' delves into the life of a daughter, illustrating a cultural practice in Vietnam where, as she grows older, the dowry or marital requirements lessen. This poetic portrayal offers us a glimpse into the customs of old Vietnamese society, shedding light on their unique traditions surrounding marriage. In contemplating this practice, we are reminded of the intrinsic value of daughters and, by extension, all women across the world. It underscores the idea that every woman is priceless, regardless of age or societal norms. 'The Cherished Daughter' serves as a compelling reminder of the importance of upholding the worth and dignity of women in every culture. Furthermore, the poem carries a profound message about maintaining our standards when choosing a life partner. It encourages us not to compromise on our values or lower our expectations, even as time passes. It implies that true love transcends superficial conditions, and a genuine partner will go to great lengths to make a lifelong commitment meaningful.

It embodies the human spirit's ability to endure and seek solace in education even in the wake of devastation. "The School Boy's Apology" serves as a testament to the innate human capacity to overcome adversity, build a hopeful future, and restore a sense of normality. In a broader context, the poem reminds us of the enduring power of hope, even in the face of the darkest circumstances. It underscores the importance of providing understanding, compassion, and support to individuals, particularly children, who have been exposed to trauma. It encourages us to create a world where opportunities for healing and growth are available to all, regardless of their past experiences. This poem's message emphasizes that hope can emerge from the most unexpected places and that, ultimately, the human spirit seeks a future that transcends the pain of the past.

Inside Submarines

The poem, "Inside the Submarine," offers a striking metaphor for the struggles and uncertainties of adult life. The comparison of human existence to odd-shaped submarines aptly illustrates the unfamiliar and often unpredictable terrain we navigate. It mirrors the complex, ambiguous nature of the world we inhabit, where we encounter unknown challenges and uncertainty daily. The idea of chasing "plastic horizons" and elusive connections in the vast ocean alludes to the universal human pursuit of dreams and aspirations that, at times, seem frustratingly out of reach due to various circumstances. This speaks to the human experience of striving for goals, even in the face of formidable odds, which is a testament to our resilience and determination. The poem's warning about slicing the earth along the equator highlights the profound interconnectedness between human actions and the environment. It reminds us of the responsibility we bear for our planet and all the living beings that share it. It's a call for mindfulness, emphasizing that our actions have consequences that can ripple through the world, affecting not just us but all life on Earth. In essence, "Inside the Submarine" encourages us to contemplate the complexity of adult life, the relentless pursuit of goals despite obstacles, and the profound impact of our actions on the world around us. It serves as a powerful reminder of our interconnectedness with the environment and the importance of responsible, mindful living for the well-being of our planet and all its inhabitants.
